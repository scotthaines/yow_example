Introduction

What

Write your own web of browser pages.

Download YOW Free Sample. Turn it into your own web of pages.

Read YOW Doc to learn how and why.

How

Never start from scratch when writing HTML browser pages. Getting the format right is too hard.

Always copy and edit a page with the format you want to put your text, pictures and links on your own browser pages.

Download YOW Free Sample to get a nice set of starter pages.

Download these three programs

  1. Amaya for editing
  2. Git for a history of changes
  3. Tortoise Git to make using Git easy.

to get the tools you need to create, edit and maintain your own browser pages.

Why

Write with hyperlinks[1] to help find documents and provide connections.

Keep a history of changes to learn from and remember changes.

It is your writing so you can decide where to publish it and what rules other should follow when using it.

zzz

Facebook and LinkedIn provide ways to write linked pages and they are fine for what they. Still, they and other online publishing services come with their own requirements, i.e., who you publish for and their rights to what you publish.You may choose to use them but they are not appropriate for all writing. All

Don't write for paper. Create webs of linked browser pages. Write .html,[2] not .txt, .odt, .docx, .ppt or or email documents.

Not surprisingly the best HTML editors are made to be HTML editors not text editors or word processors. I write with Amaya[3], a WYSIWYG[4] HTML editor sponsored by the W3C. There are many other HTML editors. BlueGriffon, Microsoft Expression Web, Adobe Dreamweaver and Froala all have something to recommend them[5].

Your web of documents will be Your Own Web (YOW) or Our Own Web (OOW) if you write in a group. Put these webs on the World Wide Web or not. You decide.

Why write with hyperlinks? Because hyperlinks in hypertext is the second advantage of electronic (digital) documents[6] over paper documents.

The big disadvantage of electronic documents is they require a device to view them.[7] All you need for text on paper are eyes, maybe glasses and the ability to read.
