Your Own Web (YOW) Example documents how to create and maintain Your Own Web of linked pages. The example, the software tools it describes and its licensing allow you to make your own static browser pages and make them public, private or proprietary. It's Your Own Web. YOW! You decide.

YOW Example emphasises your links, improvement and control over Your Own Web. YOW Example emphasises ease of use. Also it's like a social network site that does not require that your pages be on the Internet. Think Wikipedia for your stuff.

Is the content just for you, your group or your company? Fine, publish on your computer, not the Internet. Is the content for the whole world? Fine, publish on your WWW website.
